Our Story

It all started in our backyard garden, with just a handful of seeds and a beautiful dream. The Secret Garden has grown into the most beloved florists' in all of Gotham City. Our founder, Karen, believes in the magic of flowers. She is always experimenting with her designs, and has a great eye for floral arrangements.

We grow and source the most exceptional blooms from the countryside and craft arrangements that tell your unique story.
